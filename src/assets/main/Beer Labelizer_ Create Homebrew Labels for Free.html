A Beer with Punch

Strongarm got its name because I put down a home brew and forgot to tighten the fermenter tap properly. The beer was leaking out around the edge of the tap. The only way to fix things was to reach into the fermenter with my arm and tighten the tap thread. So much for a nice sterile environment.

The rest is history. My arm turned out to have just the bacteria needed for an epic pilsner and Strongarm was born.
